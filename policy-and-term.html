1. Parichay (Introduction)

Sukh Healthcare (https://sukhhealthcare.com) par aapka swagat hai. Aapki privacy hamare liye bahut mahatvapurn hai. Is Privacy Policy ka uddeshya aapko yeh batana hai ki jab aap hamari website visit karte hain ya hamari services use karte hain, toh hum aapki personal information ko kaise collect, use, aur protect karte hain.

2. Hum Kaunsi Jankari Collect Karte Hain? (Information We Collect)

Hum do tarah ki jankari collect kar sakte hain:

  • Personal Information: Jaise ki aapka naam, email address, phone number, aur appointment book karte waqt di gayi health-related basic details.
  • Non-Personal Information: Jaise ki aapka IP address, browser type, device details, aur website par aapka behaviour (cookies ke madhyam se).

3. Hum Aapki Jankari Ka Use Kaise Karte Hain? (How We Use Your Information)

Aapse collect ki gayi jankari ka upyog in kaamo ke liye kiya jata hai:

  • Medical appointments book karne aur confirm karne ke liye.
  • Aapke sawalon (queries) aur support requests ka jawab dene ke liye.
  • Healthcare services aur user experience ko behtar banane ke liye.
  • Zaroori updates, newsletters, ya health tips bhejne ke liye (agar aapne opt-in kiya hai).

4. Data Sharing aur Disclosure

Hum aapki personal information kisi bhi third-party company ko bechte (sell) nahi hain. Hum aapka data sirf trusted service providers (jaise payment gateways ya hosting partners) ke sath share kar sakte hain, jo website chalane aur aapko service dene mein hamari madad karte hain. Iske alawa, kanooni zaroorat padne par (legal requirements) hume data share karna pad sakta hai.

5. Data Ki Suraksha (Data Security)

Aapke data ki suraksha hamari prathmikta (priority) hai. Hum aapki information ko unauthorized access, loss, ya misuse se bachane ke liye standard security measures (jaise SSL encryption) ka use karte hain. Halanki, internet par 100% security ki guarantee nahi di ja sakti, par hum apni taraf se puri koshish karte hain.

6. Cookies Ka Upyog

Hamari website aapke browsing experience ko smooth aur personalized banane ke liye 'Cookies' ka use karti hai. Aap chahein toh apne browser settings mein jakar cookies ko disable kar sakte hain, lekin isse website ke kuch features theek se kaam nahi kar payenge.

7. Aapke Adhikar (Your Rights)

Aapko apni personal information ko access karne, update karne, ya delete karwane ka adhikar hai. Agar aap apni koi jankari hatwana chahte hain ya apne data se juda koi sawal puchna chahte hain, toh aap humse sampark kar sakte hain.

Hum is Privacy Policy ko samay-samay par update kar sakte hain. Jab bhi koi bada badlaav hoga, hum is page par 'Last Updated' date change kar denge. Hum aapse anurodh karte hain ki aap niyamit roop se is page ko check karte rahein.
